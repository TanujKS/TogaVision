KSAR Student Collaboration

In conjunction with the City of Saratoga's public broadcast station, KSAR15, we strive to empower students with an interest in cinematography, editing, and more with the ability to create & collaborate on projects while benefiting their community.

Engaging With The Community

Creating a more interconnected community by creating content for the citizens of Saratoga that would not be possible without volunteers.

Empowering Students

Fostering learning and hands-on experience with industry equipment & staff.

Skilled Mentorship

Hands-on projects mentored by professionals in the industry.
